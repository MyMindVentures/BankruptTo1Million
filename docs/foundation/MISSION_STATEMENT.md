## Full Mission Statement

Bankrupt to 1 Million is not a story about becoming rich. It is a story about rebuilding a life and proving that no one rebuilds alone.

Kevin and Micha are starting from rock bottom. Instead of hiding that reality, they choose to document the journey from the beginning.

The mission is to create momentum through community: hosts who offer a place to stay, developers who help build the website and apps, video editors who turn raw footage into stories, influencers who help amplify the message, angel investors who create financial breathing room, and supporters who believe in the possibility of rebuilding together.

This project combines storytelling, entrepreneurship, adventure, human connection and venture creation. The goal is not fake success, rented luxury or pretending to have everything figured out. The goal is honest progress.

Every piece of content should help move the mission forward: finding the technical partner, finding hosts, attracting creative support, building trust, making app concepts tangible, creating financial oxygen and proving that a community can help two friends break out of a vicious circle.

The road from bankrupt to one million is not measured only in money. It is measured in friendships, opportunities, resilience, shared experiences, community support, ventures launched and lives positively changed.

Everything must stay connected to this red thread: from rock bottom to momentum, from isolation to community, from ideas to tangible results, from survival to freedom.
